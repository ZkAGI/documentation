Before we dive into what's behind the OS, let us understand the AI Agent Landscape.
With the agentic paradigm capturing CT's imagination and beyond, social agents have been the first class of agents which have gained popularity. From Bully to Zerebro to Luna Virtuals, a majority of builders' (and investors') mindshare and timeshare are being poured into building better agents and building stronger infrastructure to power these agents. In some quarters, the open-source AI movement is popular. However, closed source is still dominant and such technology selection limits innovation / customizability and upgradeability.

A class of secret agents & super agents are an area of opportunity, and one ZkAGI prides itself on pioneering with the support of open innovation.
Until now, most agents have been limited to feature sets given by big tech AI APIs. Super Agents are highly powerful agents running AI models not even offered by big tech but openly available through the trenches of github!

Almost all agents born elsewhere do NOT preserve privacy on their training data or moves on-chain. In an age of AI, privacy preserves your edge. And Privacy AI enables the same outcomes for you as public AI but does not compromise your sensitive information. Being untraceable on-chain would be an agent secret power and ZkAGI will unlock this & more for you AND your agents!
ZkAGI OS is an end-to-end customizable Operating System which can be used by normies or the strongest software developers alike, allowing room for community centred innovation and incentives. The OS serves as a strong foundation which can be upgraded and modified by anyone having requisite inclination and skills. This foundational work is built on years of R & D in the global technology industry undertaken by core team which can be upgraded and modified by anyone!
