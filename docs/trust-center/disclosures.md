# Disclosures

The fine print, said plainly. We'd rather you read this now than discover it
in a contract later.

## 1. Some of this is live. Some is active R&D.

We are a research lab as much as a product company. Parts of what these docs
describe run in production today; others are under active research and
development, and capabilities land at different times. The
[Status board](/docs/trust-center/status) is the single source of truth, and
before you commit to anything we'll tell you exactly which pieces are ready
for your use case and which are still on the bench.

## 2. Configured with you, not off the shelf.

Deployments are assembled per customer and validated to your satisfaction
before go-live. That's the point of the compiler. It also means we do not
promise that every capability described in these docs exists as a general,
ready-made feature for every workload. What we deliver for you is what we
agree with you, in writing. Your agreement with zkAGI defines the guarantees,
availability and service levels that apply, and that agreement is what
governs, over anything on this site.

## 3. Your audit stays your audit.

zkAGI is not an audit firm, and our inference service has not itself been
audited or certified against HIPAA, GDPR, the EU AI Act, or any similar
framework. Compliance responsibility stays with you, the customer. Our job is
to make that job easier: receipts, attestations and documentation built to be
handed to your auditor, plus hands-on help getting ready. **We assist with
audit preparation; we do not conduct audits, issue certifications, or give
legal advice.** Regulatory mappings in these docs (for example, the table on
the [Receipts](/docs/platform/receipts) page) are orientation guidance for
your compliance team, nothing more.

## 4. Benchmarks are ours unless stated otherwise.

Figures like token-recovery bounds are produced by our own benchmark suites
under stated conditions. They are honest, repeatable, and not a substitute for
third-party validation. Where a specific deployment needs independent
measurement, we'll support it.

## 5. Peaceful use only.

zkAGI is built for peaceful, civilian use: business productivity in regulated
industries. We do not take defense or other dual-use work.

## 6. Trademarks and names.

NVIDIA, Intel TDX, AWS Nitro, Azure Confidential and similar names are
trademarks of their respective owners, referenced for comparison and
compatibility description only. OpenAI compatibility refers to API-shape
compatibility with the OpenAI client SDKs, and implies no affiliation.
